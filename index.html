About Me

Joyce Alcos was born on September 17, 2001, in the Philippines and migrated to Australia with her family in 2004. She is a dedicated professional with a passion for excellence. She currently is enrolled in a Diploma of Creative Industries and Communications at Western Sydney University, with a strong background in customer service and team management, she has consistently delivered outstanding results across various industries.

Joyce enjoys reading, hiking, and trying new restaurants. She is also passionate about learning new skills and exploring different cultures.

Career

Joyce has worked in various roles across different industries, demonstrating her adaptability and commitment to excellence. Her experience includes positions ranging from customer service to logistics and third-party logistics (3PL) warehousing.
