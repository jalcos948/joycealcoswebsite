INFO1005-Assessment 1 Arnold - Task

The unit required students to create a portfolio website showcasing their skills from class and problem solving Arnolds Scenario coding.

For this assignment Joyce was tasked with Arnold Scenario 3. Which she had to link 3 separate website pages together. Aswell as customising the design with colour and fonts after researching the colour wheel and palettes. Due to information being sourced from movies that Arnold Scwarzenegger started in such as the Terminator series, she decided that the colour gradient of Red to Black would be the most appropriate, with the white texts contrasting to balance out.

Learning Outcomes

This project helped Joyce to develop her skills and become more confident in her ability to create a cohesive website that effectively showcases her work. The process of linking multiple pages together and customizing the design with color and fonts enhanced her understanding of web development principles. Furthering her knowledge of HTML and CSS.
